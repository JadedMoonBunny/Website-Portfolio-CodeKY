About the Artist

Hello there, my name is Jade Farmer, I am a Freelance Digital Illustrator based in the United States. My specialty in art focuses primarily on visual storytelling, concept art, character design, and fantastical, but visually believable paintings.

In the past, I have worked as an animator and animation cell colorist. I am also currently enrolled in the program Code Kentucky, currently working to become a Web Developer.

I am a big fan of Dungeons and Dragons, folklore, and spooky stories. Video games and other media such as Okami, BloodBorne, Red Dead Redemption, and Night in the Woods, are really big inspirations to me in terms of art and story direction.


Skills

Adobe Photoshop, Clip Studio Paint, Paint Tool Sai, Visual Studio Code, Git and GitHub
