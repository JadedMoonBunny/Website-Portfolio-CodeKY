About The Artist

Hello, I'm Jade Farmer, a US-based artist currently living in her home state of Kentucky. I am a freelance artist who specializes in digital art and illustrations. I have worked as an animator and animation cell colorist in the past. A lot of my pieces include fantasy but visually believable, atmospheric paintings while sometimes trying to tell a story in Clip Studio Paint.

I am a big fan of Dungeons and Dragons and Studio Ghibli films. Video games such as Okami, BloodBorne, Elden Ring, Assassin's Creed, and Night in the Woods are really big inspirations to me in terms of art and story direction.
